Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Dr. Cardwell collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data Collected

  • User ID: Your unique identifier within the platform.
  • Guild ID: The identifier for the Discord server (guild) where Dr. Cardwell is used.
  • Command Usage: Records of commands you interact with, including the command name and timestamp.
  • Credit Balance: Information related to your credit balance for service usage.

2. Why Data is Collected

  • Session Management: To maintain your active sessions and provide continuous service.
  • Credit System: To manage and track your credit balance for usage of Dr. Cardwell's features.
  • Service Improvement: To understand how the bot is used and to improve its functionality and user experience.

3. How Data is Stored and Protected

  • Storage: All collected data is stored securely in our databases.
  • Protection: We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and access controls, to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your data. Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. User Rights Regarding Their Data

  • Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and operational limitations.
  • Objection: You have the right to object to the processing of your data under certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the designated support channels.
