What Can This Bot Do?

  • Massive Free Flashcard Library: Instantly access over 80 pre-built flashcard decks covering every major MCAT subject, from Biochemistry to Sociology.
  • AI-Powered Custom Decks: Go beyond our library. Instantly generate flashcards on any niche topic with Google's Gemini AI. (Premium)
  • True Semantic Search: Don't just search by topic name. Ask natural language questions like "how do cells make energy?" and find the exact flashcards you need, instantly.
  • Interactive Study Sessions: A polished, button-based UI for flipping cards, navigating decks, and redoing sets makes studying feel smooth and efficient.
  • User-Friendly Topic Browser: Use the /topics command to easily browse all available subjects with interactive, paginated menus. No more guessing topic names.
  • Patreon-Integrated Tiers: A seamless system that automatically grants premium benefits to users who support the project on Patreon.
