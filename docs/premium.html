Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in premium?

Premium tiers grant you a large monthly allowance of credits. These credits are used for advanced AI features, primarily generating custom flashcard decks on any topic you can imagine. The free version gives you full access to our entire pre-made library and search, while premium unlocks unlimited custom content creation.

How do I use my credits?

Simply use the /flashcard command with a topic that isn't in our pre-made library (e.g., /flashcard The specific role of acetyl-CoA in the Krebs cycle). The bot will use your credits to generate a brand new deck just for you.

Do my credits roll over?

No, your credit balance is refilled to your tier's amount on the first of every month. This encourages active, consistent studying!

How do I activate premium?

Just subscribe to a tier on our Patreon and make sure your Discord account is linked in your Patreon settings. Our system handles the rest automatically.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can manage your pledge directly through Patreon at any time.
